Frequently Asked Questions

Explore Our Comprehensive FAQ Section: Answers to Your Most Common Queries Await

How does the ride-sharing feature work in your app?

Our ride-sharing feature allows users to share rides with others heading in the same direction. Simply input your destination, and our app matches you with nearby users to split the cost and reduce carbon footprint.

Is it safe to share rides with strangers?

Safety is our top priority. Our app includes user verification, driver background checks, and real-time tracking for added security. Additionally, users can choose to share rides only with passengers of the same gender for added comfort and peace of mind.

Can I request a ride with a specific gender preference?

Yes, absolutely. Our app offers gender-based ride options, allowing users to request rides exclusively with drivers or passengers of the same gender, ensuring a more comfortable and secure experience.

How do I set my gender preference for rides?

It's simple! When requesting a ride, navigate to the preferences section and select your gender preference. Our app will then match you with drivers or passengers of the selected gender.

What measures are in place to prevent discrimination based on gender preferences?

We have strict policies against discrimination of any kind. Our app fosters an inclusive community where users can feel safe and respected. Any reports of discrimination are promptly investigated and addressed.

Are there any additional charges for requesting gender-based rides?

No, there are no additional charges for selecting gender-based ride options. Our goal is to provide inclusive and accessible transportation solutions for all users, regardless of their preferences.
